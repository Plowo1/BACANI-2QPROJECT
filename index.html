Overpopulation: Why it matters

Overpopulation is one of the most pressing issues facing our world today. Demand for food, water, and energy is rising at unsustainable rates, straining ecosystems and communities.

Impacts of Overpopulation (sample statistics)

ImpactGlobal Effect (%)Examples
Resource Depletion40%Freshwater shortages, deforestation
Urban Overcrowding35%Housing shortages, traffic congestion
Waste Generation25%Plastic pollution, overflowing landfills
Food Insecurity20%Rising prices, malnutrition
Environmental Damage30%Loss of biodiversity, climate change
